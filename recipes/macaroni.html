Macaroni

Description

This low fat, low calorie macaroni recipe will have you wondering where it has been all your life. Like a long lost lover you'll always make your way back to this creamy, cheesy delight, without the repercussions of indulging.

Ingredients

  • Low fat milk (250ml)
  • Hot sauce (60ml)
  • Light cream cheese (120g)
  • Low fat cheddar
  • Macaroni noodles

Recipe

  1. Add the milk, hot sauce, cream cheese, and cheddar into a pan at low heat
  2. Stir until smooth and creamy
  3. Add 500g of your favorite macaroni noodle
  4. Mix until the macaroni is fully covered
  5. Serve with a dash of salt and pepper, and enjoy!
