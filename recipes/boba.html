Boba

Description

This boba is the perfect way to enjoy your mid-day adventures! Its milky texture combined with the sugary pearls creates a colorful flavour that will leave you begging for more.

Ingredients

  • Brown sugar
  • Tapioca flour
  • Black tea bags
  • Half and half milk
  • Sweetened condensed milk

Recipe

  1. Add 1/3 cup of water and 1/4 cup of brown sugar in a pan over medium-high heat
  2. Cook until sugar dissolves and boils
  3. Add a bit of tapiocal flour and constantly stir until smooth
  4. Add half of the remaining flower in an stir vigorously until a sticky dough forms
  5. Turn off heat and add remaining flour
  6. Stir until dough forms a ball
  7. Let the dough cool slightly
  8. Place dough on a clean surface
  9. Knead until flour is fully iincorporated in the dough, and the dough is smooth. Add water or flour as needed
  10. Roll dough into long ropes about 1/4 inch thick
  11. Cut the ropes into pieces and roll them into pearls of your desired size
  12. Place the balls into a bowl with a bit of flour, and dust them
  13. Boild a large pot of water
  14. Shake excess flour off of the pearls and add them into the water
  15. Stir to separate the pearls
  16. Reduce heat to a medium-low setting and simmer gently
  17. Stir occasionally for ~20 minutes
  18. Add 1 cup of brown sugar and 1 cup of water into a pan over medium heat
  19. Cook until sugar dissolves and syrup slightly reduces
  20. Place into a heatproof bowl
  21. Once pearls have cooked, drain and rinse with cold water
  22. Add the parls to the bowl of brown sugar syrup and let sit for 30-60 minutes
  23. Add tea bags and 2 cups of water to a pot, and bring to a boil
  24. Remove pot and let tea cool to room temperature
  25. Whisk the half and half and condensed milk in a bowl until combined
  26. Add 1/3 cup of pearls to the bottom of a glass with some brown sugar syrup
  27. Add desired amount of ice
  28. Add 1/2 cup of black tea
  29. Add 3 tablespoons of milk
  30. Serve with a straw that has a wide opening, and enjoy!
